Notes:
Inspired by many of the lists shared on this discord this has been my approach to Ussura out of core set. 

Aim is to threaten kills with Yevgeni while building a board state that allows for Strong Hands cheeky claims or Indominitable will to obtain a slow Renown advantage and occasional surprise threat of Dominance Victory.

The character choice for approach deck is quite flexible but Ekaterina offers a lot of control in the first round of the game and obtaining a strong merc with filling the ranks can result in a very powerful 1st round opener. It is slightly nombo with Inspired generosity but I find that card is more useful later game for additional gas as a catch up or when needing to go first. (I might change this to shifting tides depending on further play testing).

Otherwise the deck aims to utilise strong thrust value cards and ready abilities alongside additional challenges (Iron + Velvet) to threaten strong wound potentional to the opponent. I'm still not sure whether you can fit the Muskett into Ussura given the smaller parley/hand size but it is something I continue to consider...
