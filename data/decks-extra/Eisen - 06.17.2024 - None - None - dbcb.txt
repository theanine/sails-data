Notes:
This is the deck that finished 1st and 2nd at the first Durham, NC Salon and finished 2nd at the second one.

The deck is extremely defensive, focused on blocking out of attacks and winning incremental renown wins using movement tech. It has been fine tuned against a lot of high end Montaigne and the mirror locally.

The standard character line is: Philip, Rosine, Edeline, Rena, Otto.
Rena can be played after Philip if going for an aggressive strategy (and you have a weapon).
