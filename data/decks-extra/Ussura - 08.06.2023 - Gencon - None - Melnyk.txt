Notes:
My Gencon deck.  I went 3-2 and each win was a different victory condition.  I lost a slugfest against Yevgeni and economic vs Eisen, if I recall correctly.  I started with the suggested starter deck and changed a few things.

Marooned - I need to remember about the city action on it
Ekaterina Ilyanava - MVP
Nazem Ibn Umur - I didn't get much use out of him, would consider swapping for someone else.
Improvised Weapon - I'll probably drop.  The economy for using it in dueling doesn't make up for its mediocre statline.
Matushka's Command - MVP - Locking down a location is super strong.  Sending your vulnerable guys home is also a solid play.
